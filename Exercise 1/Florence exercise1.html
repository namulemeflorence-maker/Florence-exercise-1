Uganda Martyrs Inter campus Competitions

Welcome to the Uganda Martyrs University Inter-Campus Competitions

At Uganda Martyrs University (UMU), inter-campus competitions are a cornerstone of student life.
These events bring together students from all UMU campuses to compete in sports, culture, academics, and innovation.

They promote unity, healthy competition, talent discovery, and campus pride.

Categories

Sports Competitions

Sports are the most popular part of the inter-campus events. Students compete passionately to represent their campuses and win trophies for their teams.

The following are the games for which the intercampus competitions deal in.

  • Football – the most celebrated event that draws large crowds from all campuses
  • Volleyball – both male and female teams compete in thrilling matches
  • Netball – mainly for ladies, it showcases skill, teamwork, and endurance on the court
  • Athletics – including sprints, relays, and long-distance races
  • other Games – such as table tennis, basketball, and chess

Awards and Recognition Winners receive medals, certificates, and trophies from the university administration.
The best-performing campus is recognized as the Champion of the Year and celebrated during the closing ceremony.
Outstanding individuals in sports and academics also receive special recognition.

The Inter-Campus Competitions are not only about winning but also about unity, teamwork, and talent development.
They bring life to the university and give students a chance to interact, learn, and celebrate together as one UMU family.
